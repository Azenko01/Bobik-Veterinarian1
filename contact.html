Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer veterinary services?

Yes, we have licensed veterinarians on staff who provide a range of services including check-ups, vaccinations, and minor procedures. For major surgeries, we may refer you to a specialized animal hospital.

What are your grooming services?

Our grooming services include bathing, haircuts, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and more. We cater to all breeds of dogs and cats. Appointments are recommended but walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

Do you sell prescription pet food?

Yes, we carry a variety of prescription diets for pets with special health needs. A valid prescription from a veterinarian is required for purchase.

Can I return products if my pet doesn't like them?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most unopened products with receipt. For opened food or treats that your pet didn't enjoy, we offer store credit within 14 days of purchase.
