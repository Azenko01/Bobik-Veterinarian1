About Us

Get to know our story and our team

Our Story

Bobik Veterinarian was founded in 2010 by Dr. Maria Petrova, a passionate veterinarian with a vision to create a one-stop shop for all pet needs. What started as a small clinic with a few shelves of pet supplies has grown into a comprehensive pet care center serving thousands of pet owners in the community.

Named after Dr. Petrova's childhood dog Bobik, our store embodies the love and care that every pet deserves. Over the years, we've expanded our services to include not only veterinary care but also a wide range of high-quality pet products, grooming services, and nutritional consultations.

Today, Bobik Veterinarian is proud to be a family-owned business that treats every pet that walks through our doors as if they were our own. Our team of dedicated professionals shares the same passion for animal welfare and is committed to providing the best care possible for your beloved companions.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To enhance the lives of pets and their owners by providing exceptional veterinary care, premium products, and educational resources that promote the health and happiness of all animals.

Quality

We are committed to offering only the highest quality products and services. Every item in our store is carefully selected to ensure it meets our strict standards for safety, effectiveness, and value.

Compassion

We treat every animal with the utmost care and respect. Our team understands the special bond between pets and their owners and approaches every interaction with empathy and kindness.

Education

We believe in empowering pet owners with knowledge. Through consultations, workshops, and resources, we help you make informed decisions about your pet's health and well-being.
